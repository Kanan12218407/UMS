About Placements

XYZ University offers comprehensive placement services to help students transition from academia to their professional careers. Our placement program is designed to connect students with leading employers in various industries.

  • Eligibility:
    • Open to all full-time undergraduate and graduate students.
    • Students must meet minimum GPA and academic standing requirements.
  • Process:
    • Registration and profile creation on the placement portal.
    • Resume submission and preparation workshops.
    • Interviews and selection rounds with employers.
    • Placement offers and negotiation.
  • Placement Charges:
    • Standard placement fee of $100.
    • Additional charges may apply for specialized services.
